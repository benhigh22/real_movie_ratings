Welcome!

This is a site that displays the findings of a large movie review survey. To see the Top 20 movies with the highest average rating, click the Top 20 button at the Top of this page. Or, if you're feeling adventurous, click the All Movies button to see the entire list of every movie that was reviewed! From either of these two options you can click links to find more information. Or, if you want to rate your own movie, click on the Create a New Review button to do just that! Enjoy!
